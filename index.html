Paw Track respects your privacy. All your pet data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect, transmit, store, or share any of your information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

None. Paw Track does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from you or your device.

What You Create in the App:

  • Pet profiles (name, breed, birthdate, photo)
  • Activity logs (feeding, bathroom activities, walks)
  • Health records (vet visits, vaccinations, medications)
  • Reminders and notifications
  • Photos and documents you attach

All this data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see, access, or store any of this information on our servers because we don't have any servers.

2. How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage:

Your pet data is stored directly on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync with family members.

iCloud Sync (Optional):

If you enable family sharing, your pet data syncs to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This allows you to:

  • Access your data across your own devices
  • Share specific pets with family members
  • Keep your data backed up automatically

Important: Your iCloud data is encrypted and protected by Apple. We cannot access your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs how iCloud data is handled: apple.com/legal/privacy

3. Data Sharing

We don't share your data with anyone. Ever.

Family Sharing:

When you choose to share a pet with family members through our family sharing feature, the shared pet's data is transmitted directly between your devices via iCloud. This is peer-to-peer sharing through Apple's infrastructure. We are not involved in this process.

Export Feature:

When you use our PawPack export feature, a JSON file is created on your device that you can share with whomever you choose (such as your veterinarian or AI chatbot). This is entirely under your control.

4. Third-Party Services

We don't use any third-party services. Paw Track does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Social media platforms
  • Any other third-party services

The only external service used is Apple's iCloud, which is optional and controlled by you.

6. Children's Privacy

Paw Track does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You are in complete control of your data:

  • Delete individual items: Swipe to delete any pet, log, reminder, or record
  • Delete a pet: All associated data (logs, reminders, vet visits) is also deleted
  • Delete the app: All local data is removed from your device
  • iCloud data: Deleting the app and disabling iCloud sync will remove data from iCloud

We don't retain any data because we never had access to it in the first place.

8. Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Device encryption: All local data is protected by your device's built-in encryption
  • iCloud encryption: iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No remote access: We cannot access your data remotely
  • No user accounts: No passwords to leak or accounts to hack

9. In-App Purchases

Paw Track offers a one-time purchase to unlock all features ($19.99). This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's terms and privacy policy apply: apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes

10. International Users

Paw Track is available worldwide. All data is stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account, which may be in a data center in your region. We don't transfer or process data internationally because we don't have access to your data.

11. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Export: Use the PawPack export feature to get a copy of your data
  • Delete: Delete any or all data at any time
  • Opt-out: You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of Paw Track after changes means you accept the updated policy.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

  • Legal Basis: Your consent (by using the app)
  • Data Processing: All processing happens locally on your device
  • Data Transfer: No data is transferred outside your control
  • Right to be Forgotten: Delete the app to remove all data
