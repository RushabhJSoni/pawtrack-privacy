How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage

Your pet data is stored directly on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync with family members through iCloud.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable family sharing, your pet data syncs to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This allows you to:

  • Access your data across your own devices
  • Share specific pets with family members
  • Keep your data backed up automatically

Important: Your iCloud data is encrypted and protected by Apple. We cannot access your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs how iCloud data is handled: apple.com/legal/privacy

03

Data Sharing

We don't share your data with anyone. Ever.

Family Sharing

When you choose to share a pet with family members, the shared pet's data is transmitted directly between your devices via iCloud. This is peer-to-peer sharing through Apple's infrastructure. We are not involved in this process.

Share Cards

Paw Track v2.0 lets you create shareable milestone cards (birthday cards, walk art, badge unlocks, monthly recaps, and more). These cards are generated entirely on your device. When you share a card to Instagram, iMessage, or elsewhere, you are choosing to share that specific image. We never see what you share or where you share it.

Export Features

When you use PawPack export or PDF reports, files are created on your device that you can share with whomever you choose (such as your veterinarian). When you export expense reports, the PDF is generated locally. This is entirely under your control.

04

Third-Party Services

We don't use any third-party services. Paw Track does not integrate with:

  • Analytics or telemetry services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Social media SDKs
  • Attribution or tracking frameworks
  • Any other third-party code

The only external service used is Apple's iCloud (via CloudKit), which is optional and controlled by you. Apple's StoreKit handles in-app purchases. Both are first-party Apple frameworks, not third-party services.

06

Location Data

This deserves its own section because location data is sensitive.

Paw Track v2.0 includes a GPS walk tracker. When you start a walk, the app records your route coordinates, distance, duration, pace, and steps. Here is exactly what happens with that data:

  • Stored locally: Walk route data is saved to SwiftData on your device
  • Synced via iCloud: If you use family sharing, walk data syncs through your personal iCloud like all other app data
  • Never transmitted to us: We do not receive, process, or store your location data on any server
  • No geofencing: We do not monitor your location in the background for any purpose other than active walk tracking
  • Your control: You can delete any walk from the app at any time. Revoking location permission in iOS Settings stops all tracking immediately

Walk data is only collected while you actively use the walk tracking feature. The app does not access your location at any other time.

07

Children's Privacy

Paw Track does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages. No accounts, no sign-ups, no data collection of any kind.

08

Data Retention & Deletion

You are in complete control of your data:

  • Delete individual items: Swipe to delete any pet, log, reminder, expense, diary entry, walk, or record
  • Delete a pet: All associated data (logs, reminders, vet visits, expenses, diary entries) is also deleted
  • Delete all data: Use the "Reset All Data" button in Settings to permanently erase everything
  • Delete the app: All local data is removed from your device
  • iCloud data: Deleting the app and disabling iCloud sync removes data from iCloud

We don't retain any data because we never had access to it in the first place.

09

Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Device encryption: All local data is protected by your device's built-in hardware encryption
  • iCloud encryption: iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • No remote access: We cannot access your data remotely because it is never on our infrastructure
  • No user accounts: No passwords to leak, no credentials to hack, no email addresses stored
  • Apple App Transport Security: All network communications (iCloud sync, App Store purchases) use encrypted HTTPS
10

Privacy Manifest

As required by Apple, Paw Track includes a Privacy Manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) that declares exactly which system APIs the app accesses and why:

  • UserDefaults: Used to store your app preferences and sync widget data within your device's App Group. Reason: CA92.1 (access to app-specific data)
  • File Timestamp: Used to manage photo attachments for vet records and pet profiles. Reason: C617.1 (access within app container)

No tracking APIs are used. NSPrivacyTracking is set to false.

For users in the European Union and European Economic Area:
